The Most Sacred Prayer

Namokar Mantra

णमो अरिहंताणं

I bow to the Arihantas (Conquerors)

णमो सिद्धाणं

I bow to the Siddhas (Liberated Souls)

णमो आयरियाणं

I bow to the Acharyas (Preceptors)

णमो उवज्झायाणं

I bow to the Upadhyayas (Teachers)

णमो लोए सव्व साहूणं

I bow to all the Sadhus (Saints)

"एसो पंच णमोक्कारो, सव्व पावप्पणासणो"

This five-fold salutation destroys all sins
